Detection alone was not enough for video: sponsors care about **exposure over time**. I orchestrated a **containerized Deep SORT** tracking solution that followed detected brands across frames and produced real-time exposure insights.

The system scaled to **1M+ video frames per week** with **sub-200ms** inference latency using GPU acceleration, so analytics could run continuously on incoming social video rather than as an offline batch job.
