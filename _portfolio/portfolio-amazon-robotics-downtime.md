During my co-op at **Amazon Robotics**, I worked on ops analytics for fulfillment robotic arms: why they stop, how often, and what actually fixes the problem.

I designed a **downtime monitoring system** using **AWS Lambda** and **Athena** that mapped **60%** of downtime occurrences to root causes and helped cut arm downtime by **15%**. I also built ticket triage automation with custom clustering on data integrated through **SageMaker** and **Glue**, reducing backlog on complex support tickets. Most of the work was SQL, Data Lake plumbing, and ML where it clearly beat manual triage.

### Focus
- Downtime root-cause analytics for robotic arms
- AWS Lambda, Athena, SageMaker, and Glue
- Ops-focused ML with measurable downtime reduction
