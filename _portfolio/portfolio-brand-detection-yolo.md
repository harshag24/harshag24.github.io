For sponsor analytics at **DosBro**, I developed a **YOLOv4-based brand detection framework** that processed **300K+ social media images per month** and replaced a large chunk of manual tagging work.

The system delivered **89% mAP** and cut manual labeling effort by **40%**, giving marketing teams consistent brand exposure data across Instagram and similar platforms without waiting on human review for every image.
