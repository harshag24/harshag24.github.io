During an internship at **Pheme Software**, I built an **AI-based online examination system** with anti-cheating features and integrated it into LMS platforms used by schools and training providers.

The system used **PyTesseract**-based facial recognition to monitor student behavior during exams, reaching **95% accuracy** on misconduct detection. Cheating incidents dropped **20%** and user engagement rose **40%** after rollout. There was also a recommendation layer for personalized study tips based on performance (+30% retention in early trials).

### Focus
- AI proctoring integrated with LMS platforms
- Facial recognition for exam integrity
- End-to-end exam workflow, not just a detection model
