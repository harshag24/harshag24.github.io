Our team built **Hazard View Bird**, an on-device **disaster scene parsing** system to help first responders assess damage from aerial footage. We used transfer learning with **EfficientNet-B0** to segment and classify **14 types of disaster damage** in real time.

I worked on optimization for edge deployment: pruning, quantization, and **ONNX** export for low-power hardware like **NVIDIA Jetson**, cutting processing time by **40%**. The project placed **3rd** at the Khoury College Annual Project Pitch-A-thon.

### Focus
- On-device computer vision for disaster response
- EfficientNet segmentation with edge optimization
- ONNX deployment on Jetson-class hardware
