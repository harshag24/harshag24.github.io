At **DosBro**, I deployed an **LSTM + XGBoost hybrid model** to forecast cross-platform user engagement, giving product teams a signal for when and how to push notifications instead of blasting everyone on a fixed schedule.

The hybrid beat baseline methods by **15%** on prediction accuracy and informed data-driven retention campaigns across the company's digital platforms.

### Focus
- Hybrid deep learning and gradient boosting for forecasting
- Engagement prediction across multiple platforms
